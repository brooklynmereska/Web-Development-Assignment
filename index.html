LeBron James

NBA: Cleveland Cavaliers

Bio

LeBron James is a professional basketball player in the NBA, currently playing for the Cleveland Cavaliers. He has won three NBA Championships and four NBA Most Valuable Player Awards. His first NBA season started in 2003.
